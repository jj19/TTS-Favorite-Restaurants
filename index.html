About the Restaurants

Crazy Tuna

Featuring an all-you-can-eat sushi bar, Crazy Tuna Sushi offers more than 50 sushi rolls, nigiri and sashimi in its Uptown Chandler location

El Bebo's Street Tacos

El Bebo's street tacos & hotdogs is a Taco restaurant located at San Tan Valley, Arizona. Their services include Outdoor seating, Delivery, Takeout, Dine-in.

YC's Mongolian Grill

YC's serves healthy, Mongolian-inspired stir-fry you customize yourself. Create your dish from our fresh cut ingredients, select your sauces and seasonings and then we sear it on the grill using only water and the flavors in your bowl. We're happy to help you through the process, and since each meal is personally handcrafted by you, you can make what you love and love what you make.

Phoenix Coqui

No better place to stop for delicious Puerto Rican food than Phoenix Coqui. A Puerto Rican owned food truck that will leave you wanting more and wanting to come back.
